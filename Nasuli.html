Nasuli Cold Spring


Nasuli Cold Spring, which is considered completely refreshing, is originally built and supported by the American missionaries and surrounded by green trees. The lagoon, measuring about 15 feet deep, is a great place to dive and swim, giving one a peaceful escape.



The depths that it features can support swimmers of any ability level, and a platform to dive on provides an exciting touch. There is a children section but since there is no lifeguard, it is recommended to be watched over by a guardian. The visitors have a chance to enjoy spa with small fish washing their feet, in the shadow of trees. Basic showers, toilets are well-maintained and are supplemented by cottages, convenience stores , and eateries in the area.



LOCATION

  • Nasuli Spring Resort is situated in Barangay Bangcud, about 15 to 20 minutes from Malaybalay City proper. Travelers from Cagayan de Oro or Valencia can take a bus or van along the Sayre Highway and get off near Iglesia ni Cristo Church in Bangcud.
